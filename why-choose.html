    Why We Choose Us

    CASA Interiors, established on 08-08-2020, is a dynamic team of designers dedicated to crafting extraordinary spaces that blend aesthetics and functionality. With over 7+ years of experience and more than 250 + completed projects across residential and commercial sectors, we have established ourselves as one of India’s leading interior design firms.

    Founded by Sreekanth Ummareddy and Maneesha Pydala, we focus on creating bespoke designs tailored to our clients' unique visions. We believe every space tells a story, and our innovative approach combines contemporary styles with timeless elegance. Our commitment to quality and attention to detail has earned us recognition in prestigious forums.

    Whether it’s a serene home retreat or an inspiring commercial environment, our goal is to deliver exceptional results that exceed client expectations. As we grow, we continue to push boundaries and set new benchmarks in interior design, reflecting our core values of creativity, professionalism, and customer satisfaction.
